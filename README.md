# Shift Planner

**How many hours does your life cost?**

A single-page web app for gig workers and multi-job earners to plan weekly shifts based on monthly expenses, track real earnings, and visualize where every hour goes.

🔗 **Live demo:** https://gmanthenoxus.github.io/shift-planner

## What it does

Enter your monthly bills, savings goals, and job rates. Shift Planner calculates:
- **Baseline hours/week** to cover your life
- **Weekly shifts needed** including any one-off financial goals
- **Tax-adjusted net earnings** for 8 countries (or a flat custom rate)
- **Visual breakdown** showing exactly where each earned hour goes—rent, savings, taxes, etc.

Log each week's actual shifts. Shift Planner shows you what you earned, whether you hit your target, and which obligations you covered in priority order.

## Quick start

1. **Set your location** — picks your currency and tax model
2. **Add your jobs** — hourly rate, typical weekly hours, pension %
3. **List monthly outgoings** — bills, savings, spending, tagged by category
4. **Set a working ceiling** — max days/week and hours/day you'll accept
5. **Add goals (optional)** — lump-sum targets with deadlines
6. **Log your shifts** — track start, end, breaks as you work
7. **Hit "New week"** — banks the week and shows what you covered

Your data stays in your browser. Export/import JSON anytime.

## Features

- **Tax calculator** for UK, Ireland, Germany, France, Netherlands, Spain, US, Canada, Australia (or flat custom %)
- **Multi-job blending** — weighted hourly rate across jobs
- **Pension tracking** — per-job contribution rates
- **Priority coverage** — see which bills/goals your earnings reached first
- **Hour breakdown** — donut chart showing what one earned hour pays for
- **Goal tracking** — lump-sum targets spread across weeks
- **Shift logging** — calculate pay per shift automatically
- **History** — track past weeks and coverage
- **Export/import** — backup and restore your data as JSON
- **Responsive** — works on mobile, tablet, desktop
- **No account needed** — all data stays local in your browser

## Browser support

Modern browsers with ES6 support and localStorage. Works offline once loaded.

## Disclaimer

Tax models are simplified effective-rate estimates and ignore many personal factors (allowances, credits, student loans, overtime rules). This is planning guidance, not a payslip. Always verify with official sources before making financial decisions.
